About Me

I am a proud native of Detroit Michigan. Raised by my grandmother before her death in 1996. I unfortunetley was influenced by the streets early in my teenager years. After a brief run end with the law I turned my life around and finished high school with awards and honors. After failing the 9th grade, I refocused my life, spiritual relationship with Jesus Christ.

I am an entreprenuer! After earning $80 bucks in 1995 from shoveling snow, I was ready to business. I have looked forward to this opportunity throughout my whole life. With all the experiences and opportunities have prepared me to lead and coach individuals and organizations into phenomenal leaders.
